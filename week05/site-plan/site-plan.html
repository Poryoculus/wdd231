Site Plan - Agaromi

Site Name

Agaromi

Agaromi is a warm, elegant name inspired by love and tradition, representing both the uniqueness of my mother’s handcrafted art and the care she gives her students. It’s short, memorable, and brandable for her growing business.

Optional domain availability: agaromi.art

Site Purpose

This website serves as the official online presence of Agaromi. Visitors can subscribe to her handcraft classes, view a dynamic gallery of her work, learn about her story. The site includes animated elements for interactivity.

Scenarios

  • How do I sign up for a class at Agaromi?
  • Where can I see photos of past projects and crafts?
  • What type of classes i can attend?

Color Schema

  • #7a4e1d (Dark Brown): Primary color for navigation bars and section dividers, representing a grounded and elegant aesthetic.
  • #d6ad60 (Golden Sand): Secondary color used for buttons and hover effects to bring a vibrant yet classy highlight.
  • #f3e2d8 (Light Peach): Used for lighter backgrounds and footer areas to create a soft and cohesive look.

Typography

  • Tinos: Used for body text, giving it a clean, readable, and classic style.
